The Monkees

We love music!

We are an American rock / pop Band. We were formed in Los Angeles in 1965 by Bob Rafelson and Bert Schneider for the American television series The Monkees which aired from 1966 to 1968. The band's music is supervised by producer Don Kirshner, backed by the songwriting duo of Tommy Boyce and Bobby Hart. The Monkees have sold more than 75 million records worldwide making them one of the biggest selling groups of all time with international hits, including "Last Train to Clarksville", "Pleasant Valley Sunday", "Daydream Believer", and "I'm a Believer".
